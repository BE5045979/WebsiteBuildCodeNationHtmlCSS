Unit 2 1.5

The digital millennium copyright act. (See Unit1-1.7 [9]) Which protects intellectual properties of creators and businesses.

As we touched on previously, this law means that the media used on your project is original media created by yourself or a creator, or even royalty free. You cannot use media or content created by others without permission. It's in place to protect content creators from intellectual theft and piracy.

On the site ive created all the images and video content are royalty free or have been made in G.I.M.P 2.0 software. I decided to go this way as using copyrighted material can lead to legal issues with the content creators.
As there is no form to input data, there's no conflicts with data protection laws or GDPR.
The site also doesn't send out any emails to users therefore does not conflict with The Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations i discussed in Unit1-1.7.
