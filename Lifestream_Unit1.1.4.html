Unit 1 1.4

With digital projects there can be a few issues that can affect a site. One of them as we have touched on earlier is Copyright Infringement. [3] When using media on your project, ensure that its original content or royalty free.
(https://en.wikipedia.org › wiki › Royalty-free)
You wouldn't want to get into legal trouble down the line to save money or time (both of which you could lose because of this.) this can sometimes lead to sites being taken down.

Another possibility of failure could arise from simple poor design. Users are more likely to use a site that looks good and is easy to use. (except Reddit IMO) Bad promotion can lead to a dip in traffic or none at all if you do not market it right. You can use social media to advertise or promotional events to draw in more new users.
Making your site accessible to the masses is important, will it work on tablets and mobile the same as pc? If the answer is no then that's something to change immediately.
Being mobile friendly is something expected from modern sites and will push you down the rankings when being searched in search engines like Chrome, Firefox or Edge.[4]

Finally having pointless, useless or just poor content can affect your site, you wouldn't visit a gallery without art would you? (unless you're into that kinda thing).
